About the Project

TPIS is a fully developed healthcare platform that digitizes patient information for Neema Hospital. This system has replaced outdated paper-based records, significantly reducing errors and enhancing service delivery.

The solution integrates Electronic Health Records (EHR), Practice Management, and a Master Patient Index (MPI)â€”ensuring all patient data is secure, accessible, and efficiently managed.

Core Features

  • Electronic Health Records (EHR): Secure storage of patient visits, lab results, and treatment history.
  • Practice Management: Automated appointment scheduling, billing, and reminders.
  • Master Patient Index (MPI): Prevents duplication by uniquely identifying each patient.
  • Patient Portal: Patients can view health records, book appointments, and receive updates online.

Key Benefits

  • Improved healthcare quality and service speed.
  • Reduced paperwork and manual data entry.
  • Enhanced patient satisfaction via online services.
  • Time and cost savings for hospital administration.
  • Greater data accuracy and compliance with health standards.
